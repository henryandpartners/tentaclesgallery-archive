Residency program for Creator at YET SPACE, Bangkok

Period: February 1 – December 31, 2016

(กรุณาอ่านภาษาอังกฤษ)

YET Space residency is a collaboration between Tentacles and YET Space  community. Located in a shop house in Charansanitwong Soi 11 on Bangkok Yai district, Thonburi side of Bangkok surrounded by chaotic energy where you can find plenty of local specialties and food stalls. The perfect place where cost of living are cheaper than central Bangkok and here where you can find all sort of supplies for  production eg. stationary, wood, metal, plastic, garment.  A place where you can connect with Thai creators and concentrate on your own activities.   It consists of 1 bedrooms, 2 share studios, share toilet.

(Creator should seeks funds from their own country for Airfares, Living Allowances, Insurance, Production, Travel, Etc.)
