Argya Dhyaksa Nindita

He has interest in beauty in ugliness and silliness. Most of his ceramics forms are rough, ugly chaotically imperfect maybe this is wabi sabi excuse for him that there is a beauty behind the impermanent and imperfection

With the idea of wabi sabi in mind. We may learn a great (inner) satisfaction in a humble moments. He use pinch technique for personal touch on my ceramic to get an intimacy irregularity and unpretentious.

He often using forms like toys, organic ornament,

and random things he found on the internet as his perspective on his banal daily life that he lives, He believe that world is just an amusement, diversion and adornment.

Somethimes he add wordplay like anagram spoonerism, spoof of something, slip of tougue, misheard quotes to tell that all his whole life is a joke.

He was Artist in Residency at Tentacles from 1 February – 30 March 2020
