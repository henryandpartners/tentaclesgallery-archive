Scott’s practice is an ongoing investigation within the expanded field of painting. His work considers place/non-place and transition/flux within urban environments, and how cultural identities influence ones understanding of place. Scott’s expanded painting practice explores

ideas relating to reduction, construction/deconstruction, often using found materials to create spatially ambiguous environments that cannot be realised within a single work.

He will be Artist in Residency at Tentacles from 30 January 2020 – 29 March 2020

“18 Minute Walk” is an installation work inspired by Scott’s daily experience of “getting a coffee”. Exploring ideas associated with reductive painting, deconstruction, new materials and painting as a hybridised practice he has produced a series of works loosely motivated by the architectural forms, grids, grills and manhole covers located between tentacles residence and The Up Rama 3.
