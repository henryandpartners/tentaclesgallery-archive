Ane Fabricius Christiansen works with sculpture, installation, video and photography, with clay as her main material. Her approach is both experimental and conceptual. She is based in Kolding, Denmark and has exhibited at galleries and museums in both Denmark and internationally. She is a member of the prize-winning art group VERSUS..

Since 2010 Ane Fabricius Christiansen has been based in Kolding in Denmark with atelier at the artist studio Formfabrikken. The last couple of years she has found interest in Thailand, which in spring 2016, leads to an artist residency at Chulalongkorn University in Bangkok.
